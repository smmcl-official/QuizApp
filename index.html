Creating Quiz Questions: A Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these instructions to format your quiz questions correctly:

1. The Question:

Start each question with the keyword Question: followed by your question text.

Example: 

Question: What is the capital of Australia?

2. The Choices:

On a new line, use the keyword Choices: to begin listing the possible answers.

Each choice should:

  • Start on a new line.
  • Begin with a single capital letter identifier (A, B, C, or D) followed by a closing parenthesis.
  • Contain the choice text.

Example: 

Choices:
  A) Sydney
  B) Melbourne
  C) Canberra
  D) Perth

3. The Correct Answer:

On a new line, use the keyword Correct Answer: followed by the letter identifier and the text of the correct answer (exactly as it appears in the choices).

Example: 

Correct Answer: C) Canberra

4. Separating Questions:

Place three hyphens (---) on a new line to separate each question from the next.

Complete Template:

Question: What is the capital of France?
Choices:
  A) London
  B) Paris
  C) Berlin
  D) Rome
Correct Answer: B) Paris
---
Question: What is the highest mountain in the world?
Choices:
  A) K2
  B) Kangchenjunga
  C) Mount Everest
  D) Lhotse
Correct Answer: C) Mount Everest
---
